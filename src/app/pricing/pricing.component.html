Website Components

To have your website up and running you will need three components: domain, hosting, and code.

Domain

Name for your website which is also called an URL. For example www.example.com

Price starts at: $10-12 per year

Depends on the seller and extension (.com, .org, etc).

Hosting

Hosting provider is a fancy name for an online place where your website is stored and processed.

Average price: $10 per year

Depends on the hosting provider and duration of the agreement.

Code

Website itself which is files with the code.

Price starts at: $75

Depends on the complexity.

Interactive Websites

Interactive websites are not just for information. Such websites let you post new articles, get feedback or comments from your users, process payments.

There are two ways to build an interactive website: using WordPress or from scratch.

WordPress

WordPress is a prebuilt platform that saves effort and time for me, therefore it saves money for you. However, the functionality and design of your website will be limited to what plugins are available. If you have standard requirements for your website, WordPress is an option for you.

Custom Built

If you plan to expand your website, add new features and functions in the future, or you have some particular design in mind, you might want to build your website from scratch using programing languages. That way you can fully customize it to meet any of your requirements.

Blog Style Website

Description: If you want to regularly update your users or customers with new information like recipes, articles, your company news, then blog style website is for you.

Time: 3-5 days.

Cost: Starts at $100 for WordPress based website.
